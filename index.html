What we offer

Japheth's Powered Fitness is your very own boutique fitness center designed to provide a first-class fitness experience. We aim to deliver effective, convenient workouts in our state-of-the-art studio using market-leading fitness equipment.
We tailor unique packages for our members and track their progress using innovative technology. For the discerning client, you will love our one-of-a-kind VIP locker suite, with next-level hospitality.

High-Intensity Interval Training

Short bursts of intense exercise alternated with brief rest periods, known for maximizing calorie burn and boosting metabolism

Correctional Therapy Exercise

Cycoreflex

Pilates Bootcamp

Intensive Pilates-based workouts focusing on core strength, flexibility, and body conditioning

Yoga

Classes focused on breathing, flexibility, and strength while promoting relaxation and stress relief.

Barre
